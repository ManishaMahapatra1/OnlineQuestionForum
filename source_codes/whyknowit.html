Why know IT



The main objective of why know IT was to create a platform for asynchronous communication among users. They are seen as online collaborative spaces were diverse set of people converge and share knowledge. Due to this huge repository of knowledge contributed by people from all over the world, online forums can be effective sources of learning. They were also seen as enablers of blended and constructivist learning.

Know IT forum provide a place for users to ask specific questions and receive direct answers. They're typically more organized than communities and contain answers based on experience and knowledge.

This forums provide an excellent platform for learning and connecting participants across the globe. Users can register for free and post their questions related to any programming concepts such as programming languages, databases, etc.
